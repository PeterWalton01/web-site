My Background

Welcome to my portfolio website.


I am a highly motivated, experienced technical manager with a long history of successful projects, delivering excellent customer solutions. Many of these were integration projects and involved high levels of innovation to deliver new services. A good number of these projects were strategically significant and supported large renewable revenue streams amounting to millions of pounds per year. My key management skills include team leadership, motivation, and direction. During the development of new solutions, I believe that a keen understanding of customer requirements and the ability to envisage processes and solutions to meet these are essential. My technical skills include a strong understanding of technologies and how they lead to excellent solutions requiring low levels of administration and maintenance. My main objective when working on projects is to support a shock-free project environment. This allows the sponsors to track progress with a light touch, leaving them free to focus on other activities.
